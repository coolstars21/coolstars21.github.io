COOL STARS 21

4-9 July 2022, Toulouse, France

The "Cambridge Workshops of Cool Stars, Stellar Systems and the Sun" are held biennially and have evolved to be the premier conference series for cool star research.

The conference will be held in hybrid format. Registration and abstract submission are open!

 
SOC and LOC plan the following important dates:
  • January 12th 2022: list of selected splinter sessions announced
  • February 1st 2022: deadline for travel grant support
  • March 6th 2022: abstract submission deadline
  • March 15th 2022: announcement of selected grantees
  • March 30th 2022: announcement of selected talks (plenary sessions)
  • April 06th 2022: end of early-bird registration fees
  • May 18th 2022: end of regular registration fees
  • June 27th 2022: poster submission deadline and end of registration

COOL STARS 21

22 - 26 June 2020, Toulouse, France

The "Cambridge Workshops of Cool Stars, Stellar Systems and the Sun" are held biennially and have evolved to be the premier conference series for cool star research.

Registration and abstract submission are now open!

 

CORONAVIRUS INFORMATION:

The organizers are monitoring daily the evolution of coronavirus worldwide and how this may affect international travel and large meetings. At the moment, most effective travel bans do not have a known duration and may be lifted before the date of the CS21 conference and as of today (March 11th) we have not been asked by the French government or the organizing Research institutes to cancel/postpone the conference. As a consequence, CS21 is still scheduled to be held as planned on the week of 22nd of June 2020.

We, therefore, encourage everyone to submit abstracts and register. Any registration will be 100% refunded if (a) the meeting has to be cancelled or (b) if you decide to cancel your participation less than 60 days after your registration.

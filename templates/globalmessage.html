COOL STARS 21

5-9 July 2022, Toulouse, France

The "Cambridge Workshops of Cool Stars, Stellar Systems and the Sun" are held biennially and have evolved to be the premier conference series for cool star research.

Due to the sanitary situation, the CS21 conference is postponed to 5-9 July 2022. Please join the pre-registered mailing list to have updates. In the meanwhile, we are happy to announce that we will have a CS20.5 virtual meeting from 2 to 4 March, 2021.
